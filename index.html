About Me

 

My journey into programming started with a simple question: How do computers make things move on a screen?

One of my first encounters with programming was with the Microsoft Small Basic language from junior high school which fueled my interest in making computers do different actions. I found it fascinating how how a couple lines of code can transform the way we interact with technology.

It was only a matter of time until websites piqued my interest. The idea of having your own website- a digital presence that you can personalize with great freedom; in the world wide web, is something I thought was cool. It was here where my journey with web development started.

From there, I discovered the steps to create a website. However, at the time, it was something I struggled with and had difficulties trying to learn it on my own. At some point, I had to call it quits. But, I did not completely given up on it. It had always been at the corner of my mind.

And here we are, my interest rekindled and a newfound excitement burning within. Now, I am completely absorbed in web development; driven by enthusiasm and passion to learn and grow. Currently, I am at the starting line of my web development journey, making my first steps in pursuit of self-improvement and excellence.
